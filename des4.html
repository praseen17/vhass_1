Cybersecurity for Teenagers

Stay safe, secure, and smart in the digital world!

Teen-Friendly | Interactive Learning | Social Media Safety | Gaming Security

About This Course

This isn't your boring computer class! Learn cybersecurity through games, real teen scenarios, and cool tech stuff. We'll show you how to protect your social media, gaming accounts, and phone while still having fun online.

Gaming Security

Protect your Fortnite, Roblox, and other gaming accounts from hackers

Phone Safety

Keep your phone and apps secure from creeps and scammers

Social Media Smarts

Post safely on Instagram, TikTok, Snapchat and more

What You'll Learn

We'll cover all the digital safety stuff you actually need to know:

Level 1: Locking Down Your Life
  • Create hacker-proof passwords (that you can actually remember)
  • Two-factor authentication for gamers
  • Spotting fake login pages for Robux/V-Bucks scams
  • What to do when your account gets hacked
Level 2: Social Media Survival
  • Dangerous DM scams on Instagram/Snapchat
  • Spotting fake profiles and catfishing
  • What NOT to share on TikTok
  • Dealing with online creeps and cyberbullies
Level 3: Public WiFi & Phone Safety
  • Is Starbucks WiFi safe? (The truth might surprise you)
  • Spotting fake charging stations
  • App permissions - what's safe to allow?
  • Location sharing dangers
Level 4: Viruses & Free Stuff Scams
  • "Free Fortnite skins" - too good to be true?
  • Spotting fake app downloads
  • When mods and cheats turn dangerous
  • What to do if your phone gets infected
Level 5: Becoming Cyber-Smart
  • Private browsing - what it really does
  • Sextortion scams and how to handle them
  • Digital footprint cleanup
  • How to tell your friends their account got hacked

Is This For You?

If you:

  • Have a smartphone or gaming account
  • Use Instagram, TikTok, Snapchat, or Discord
  • Want to keep your accounts from getting hacked
  • Like free stuff but don't want viruses
  • Want to learn cybersecurity skills that could get you a cool job later

Questions You Might Have

Is this going to be like school?

No way! No lectures or tests. We use memes, games, and real examples from platforms you actually use. Learn by doing cool challenges.

Do I need to be good at computers?

Nope! If you can use a phone, you can do this. We start with the basics and make everything super clear.

Will my parents get reports?

Only if you want them to! This is your private learning space. But we do give you stuff to show them if you want to teach them something.

Is there a group or forum?

Yes! Our moderated Discord server lets you chat with other teens learning cybersecurity, ask questions, and share tips.
