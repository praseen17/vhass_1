Course Syllabus

The Advanced Security Testing course includes 18 comprehensive modules covering the latest security assessment techniques and tools used by elite security teams.

Module 1: Introduction to Bug Bounty & Platforms
  • Why companies offer bug bounties
  • Types of vulnerabilities rewarded
  • Risk quantification methodologies
  • Popular Platforms: HackerOne, Bugcrowd, Synack, Intigriti, YesWeHack
  • Creating a profile & understanding policies
Module 2:Reconnaissance & Information Gathering
  • Target Selection & Scope Analysis
  • Passive Recon: WHOIS, ASN, Google Dorking
  • Active Recon: Subdomain Enumeration (Subfinder, Assetfinder, Amass)
  • IP Range, Port & Service Scanning (Nmap, Naabu)
  • Tools: Nmap, Subfinder, HTTPx, Gau, JSParser, Waybackurls
  • Lab Task: Full recon on a demo target
Module 3: Vulnerability Discovery Techniques
  • Common Vulnerabilities
  • Parameter Fuzzing using ffuf, Kiterunner
  • Testing with Burp Suite (Community/Pro)
  • Custom wordlists & techniques
  • XSS,SQL Linjection,IDOR,Clickjacking
Module 4:Real-World Exploitation & Advanced Techniques
  • Bypassing WAFs
  • Business Logic Testing Techniques
  • Bug chaining and privilege escalation
  • Chaining Vulnerabilities for Higher Impact
Module 5:Reporting & Responsible Disclosure
  • How to write a professional report
  • Proof of Concept (PoC) creation (screenshots, videos)
  • Severity rating (CVSS/CWE)
  • Client-side security testing
  • Do's and Don'ts of responsible disclosure
Module 6:Bug Bounty Mindset, Strategy & Career
  • Choosing the right programs & bugs to hunt
  • Staying up to date with new techniques
  • Defense evasion techniques
  • Lateral movement strategies
  • Building a reputation & personal branding

Frequently Asked Questions

How does this differ from standard penetration testing courses?

This program focuses on advanced techniques used in real-world sophisticated attacks, covering modern architectures and defense bypass methods that go beyond standard penetration testing methodologies. We emphasize multi-vector attacks and simulating advanced persistent threats.

What certification will I receive upon completion?

You'll receive the VHASS Certified Advanced Security Tester (CAST) certification, recognized by our industry partners. The program also prepares you for advanced vendor-neutral certifications like OSCE and CISSP-ISSAP.

What's the hands-on component like?

The course includes 90 hours of hands-on labs featuring complex, multi-stage attack scenarios in our cyber range environment. You'll work with modern enterprise architectures, cloud environments, and sophisticated defensive measures.

Is there a capstone project?

Yes, the course culminates with a 2-week red team engagement simulation where you'll plan and execute a comprehensive security assessment against a realistic enterprise environment, then present your findings to a panel of security leaders.

What tools will we use in the course?

You'll work with industry-standard tools like Burp Suite Pro, BloodHound, Cobalt Strike, and custom tooling, along with developing your own scripts and tools for specialized testing scenarios.
