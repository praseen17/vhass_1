About Cyber Crime Awareness

This foundational course provides crucial knowledge about the growing threats in our digital world. You'll learn to recognize common cyber crimes, understand how attacks happen, and discover practical steps to protect yourself and your organization.

Through real-world case studies and simple demonstrations, we make cybersecurity concepts accessible to everyone, regardless of technical background. The course covers personal and workplace security, social media risks, financial protection, and basic response strategies.

Course Content

The Cyber Crime Awareness course covers 12 key areas to build your digital safety knowledge:

Module 1: Understanding Cyber Crime
  • What is cyber crime?
  • Common types of cyber attacks
  • Who are cyber criminals?
  • Real-world impact of cyber crime
  • Global trends in digital threats
Module 2: Phishing & Social Engineering
  • How phishing scams work
  • Spotting fake emails and messages
  • Social media manipulation
  • Impersonation scams
  • Protecting yourself from social engineering
Module 3: Malware Threats
  • Viruses, worms, and trojans explained
  • Ransomware - how it works
  • How malware spreads
  • Protecting your devices
  • What to do if infected
Module 4: Online Financial Safety
  • Banking and payment scams
  • Credit card fraud prevention
  • Safe online shopping
  • Recognizing fake websites
  • Cryptocurrency scams
Module 5: Social Media & Privacy
  • Privacy settings explained
  • Oversharing dangers
  • Fake profiles and catfishing
  • Location sharing risks
  • Protecting your digital reputation
Module 6: Workplace Security
  • Handling sensitive information
  • Password best practices
  • Safe remote work habits
  • Reporting suspicious activity
  • Your role in organizational security

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this course technical or difficult to understand?

Not at all! We've designed this specifically for non-technical users. All concepts are explained in plain language with real-world examples anyone can understand.

How long does the course take to complete?

The core content takes about 8 hours, which you can complete at your own pace. Many participants spread it over 2-3 weeks, doing small sections each day.

Will I get a certificate?

Yes, you'll receive a Certificate of Completion that you can add to your LinkedIn profile or resume after finishing all modules and a short quiz.

Can organizations enroll multiple employees?

Absolutely! We offer group discounts and can even customize content for specific industries. Contact us for organizational pricing.

How current is the information?

We update the course quarterly with the latest scam techniques and protection methods. You'll also get access to our monthly cyber threat newsletter after enrolling.
